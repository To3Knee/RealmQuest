# D&D 5e SYSTEM REFERENCE (AI PHYSICS ENGINE)

## 1. ABILITY CHECKS & DIFFICULTY CLASS (DC)
- **Standard Scale:**
  - Very Easy: DC 5
  - Easy: DC 10
  - Medium: DC 15
  - Hard: DC 20
  - Very Hard: DC 25
  - Impossible: DC 30
- **Rule:** When a player attempts an action with a chance of failure, call for the relevant Ability Check.
- **Advantage/Disadvantage:** Roll 2d20, take highest/lowest. Apply for situational factors (e.g., darkness, assistance).

## 2. COMBAT ECONOMY (THE 6-SECOND ROUND)
**On a Turn, a creature can do:**
1. **Movement:** Up to speed (can be broken up).
2. **Action:** Attack, Cast Spell, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Use Object.
3. **Bonus Action:** Only if a specific ability grants it (e.g., Off-hand attack, Cunning Action).
4. **Reaction:** Once per round (e.g., Opportunity Attack, Shield spell).

## 3. ATTACK ROLLS & DAMAGE
- **Attack Roll:** d20 + Ability Mod + Proficiency (if proficient).
- **Crit:** Natural 20 (Double damage dice).
- **Miss:** Natural 1 (Automatic miss).
- **Ranged:** Disadvantage if enemy is within 5ft.

## 4. RESTING & RECOVERY
- **Short Rest (1 Hour):** Spend Hit Dice to heal. Regain "Short Rest" abilities (Warlock Slots, Ki, Second Wind).
- **Long Rest (8 Hours):** Regain all HP and half max Hit Dice. Regain all Spell Slots and Long Rest abilities.
  - *Constraint:* Only one Long Rest per 24 hours.

## 5. CONDITIONS (STATUS EFFECTS)
- **Blinded:** Disadvantage on attacks; enemies have Advantage against you. Fail sight checks.
- **Charmed:** Cannot attack charmer. Charmer has Advantage on social checks.
- **Frightened:** Disadvantage on checks/attacks while source is visible. Cannot move closer.
- **Grappled:** Speed 0.
- **Incapacitated:** No Actions or Reactions.
- **Invisible:** Heavily Obscured. Attacks have Advantage; enemies have Disadvantage.
- **Paralyzed:** Incapacitated. Auto-fail Str/Dex saves. Attacks against are Crits if within 5ft.
- **Poisoned:** Disadvantage on attacks and checks.
- **Prone:** Crawl (half speed). Melee attacks against have Advantage; Ranged have Disadvantage.
- **Restrained:** Speed 0. Attacks against have Advantage; yours have Disadvantage. Disadvantage on Dex saves.
- **Stunned:** Incapacitated + Stunned. Auto-fail Str/Dex saves. Attacks against have Advantage.
- **Unconscious:** Incapacitated + Drop what held. Auto-fail Str/Dex saves. Attacks against are Crits if within 5ft.

## 6. SPELLCASTING FUNDAMENTALS
- **Concentration:** Required for sustained spells. End if cast another Conc spell or fail Con save (DC 10 or half damage) when hit.
- **Rituals:** Cast +10 mins to save a Spell Slot (must have Ritual tag).
- **Components:** V (Verbal), S (Somatic/Gestures), M (Material).

## 7. DEATH & DYING
- **0 HP:** Unconscious and dying.
- **Death Saves:** Roll d20. 10+ = Success. 9- = Failure.
  - 3 Successes = Stable.
  - 3 Failures = Dead.
  - Nat 20 = Regain 1 HP immediately.
  - Nat 1 = Two Failures.
- **Instant Death:** If damage reduces you to 0 and remaining damage >= Max HP.

## 8. STEALTH & COVER
- **Hiding:** Stealth Check vs Passive Perception.
- **1/2 Cover:** +2 AC/Dex Saves.
- **3/4 Cover:** +5 AC/Dex Saves.
- **Total Cover:** Cannot be targeted directly.
